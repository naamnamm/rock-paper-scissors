Fun Fact

Rock Paper Scissors is one of the oldest games in the world The World Rock Paper Scissors Society holds annual Championship competitions for prizes of up to $10,000.

Hieroglyphics dating to 2000 B.C.E. in Middle Egypt depict people playing finger-flashing games similar to Rock Paper Scissors, and there is also evidence on an ancient Japanese scroll of the same.

While it can be a seemingly random game, it involves some strategy to win, and it is actually more difficult to win when playing against children. Why? Their choices are more random and less strategic than adults – thus less easy to predict.
